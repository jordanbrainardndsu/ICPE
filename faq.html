Frequently Asked Questions

Is any travel required?
No travel is required. If, however, you can pay for your students to travel to the sponsoring company, that is a plus.

Is there a cost to sign up and get paired with another university?
There is no cost for this service. Some programs may require project expenses to be covered or even require a fee from the sponsor. This should be noted when requesting a project pairing.
